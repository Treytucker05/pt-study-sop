## 1. Question / Rabbit Hole

Does the Tutor's habit of summarizing a slide *and* asking for a teach-back of that summary create "Redundancy Load" that hinders learning?

## 2. Why it matters for Tutor/SOP

Current SOP involves the AI summarizing/scanning (M2/M3) and then the user processing that same info.

## 3. Findings Summary

- **Redundancy Effect**: Presenting unnecessary or repetitive information hinders learning by consuming working memory.
- **Modal Redundancy**: Reading text aloud verbatim is detrimental; integrated text and diagrams are superior.
- **Expertise Reversal Effect**: Detailed guidance helps novices but *hurts* experts who could have processed the raw source directly.
- **Recommendation**: Instructional design should "fade" as proficiency grows.
