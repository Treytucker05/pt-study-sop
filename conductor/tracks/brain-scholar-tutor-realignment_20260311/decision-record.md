## Primary Product Decision

Trey’s Study System is a 3-part product:

- **Brain** = learner-model engine
- **Scholar** = direct research partner
- **Tutor** = live protocol-led teacher

This replaces page-first drift as the primary product framing.

## Locked Decisions

1. **Premium individual first**
   - Build the best individual product first.
   - Do not distort the architecture around institution-first admin complexity yet.

2. **Archive can restore repeated lost capabilities**
   - Archive does not overrule active canon.
   - Archive can restore a capability when it appears repeatedly and matches current owner intent.

3. **Brain owns learner modeling**
   - Brain captures study telemetry.
   - Brain emits evidence-backed profile claims, fit signals, confidence, contradictions, and hybrid archetypes.

4. **Scholar is learner-facing**
   - Scholar can ask the learner targeted questions.
   - Scholar can research the web and synthesize cited findings.
   - Scholar remains non-teaching for course content.

5. **Tutor is the only live teaching engine**
   - Tutor stays protocol-led, chain-bound, and governed by SOP/runtime rules.

6. **No direct Brain-to-Tutor steering**
   - Brain cannot directly author Tutor pedagogy.
   - Any adaptation must pass through a bounded Scholar strategy envelope.

7. **Archetypes are visible and challengeable**
   - Brain archetypes are explanatory summaries, not manual labels.
   - The learner can challenge and calibrate them.
   - Manual freeform override is not the default.

8. **Commercial trust is part of the product**
   - Explainability, evidence, confidence, learner control, export/privacy, and auditability are core product requirements.

## Immediate Consequences

- Active canon/docs that contradict these decisions must be updated in Wave 0.
- Scholar runtime must eventually gain real research capability instead of prompt-only research language.
- Brain MVP must ship as a learner-facing profile system, not just a hidden telemetry layer.
- Tutor adaptation cannot be implemented until the bounded strategy envelope is frozen.
